Caste Opinions in India

The caste system in India is deeply rooted in society. Many believe that castes arose at the time of the Vedic period, but the caste system was not fully formed until the 18th century. The caste system is a social hierarchy that divides people into four main groups: Brahmins, Kshatriyas, Vaishyas, and Shudras. The caste system is based on the Hindu religion, and it is believed that the caste system was created by the Hindu gods. The caste system is still prevalent in India today, and it is a major factor in the country's politics.
